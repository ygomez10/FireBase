Your Journey Comes Here


Our Journey is a project to present the stories of Immigrants and Refugees who have overcome many obstacles to achieve a better life by coming to the United States. We want to present these individual stories in a way where people can exhibit empathy and understand certain struggles endured in order to get to where they are today. Migration is one thing, but acceptance and adapting is another. Let your stories be heard.
