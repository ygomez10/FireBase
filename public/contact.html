Do you have a story to Share?

If you have a story of the struggles involved in your transition to the United States you can submit your story here for a chance to have it transformed into a game. By sharing your journey you are exposing others to the harsh realities involved in coming to an entirely new country.The main goal of this project is to build empathy, create understanding and showcase your strength.

Empathy

In the current political state empathy is needed on all sides to bridge the gap between the unkown and the struggles of immigrants.

Understanding

In order to create a deeper understanding of individuals on all sides we must show the stories of the most couragous individuals.

Showcase

What better way to create empathy and understanding than to showcase the very individuals who made the journey themselves?
